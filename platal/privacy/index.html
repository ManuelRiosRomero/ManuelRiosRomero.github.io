Platal — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Platal is a personal finance tracker that runs entirely on your device. Your privacy is simple because Platal is built to keep your data with you.

What we collect

Nothing. Platal does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, no third-party SDKs, no servers, and no account to create.

Where your data lives

All of your information — expenses, income, budgets, categories, accounts, and settings — is stored locally on your device. It never leaves your device unless you choose to export it.

Backups you control

Platal lets you export a backup file (optionally password-encrypted). You choose where it goes — for example the Files app, iCloud Drive, or AirDrop. These exports are entirely under your control; the developer never receives them and cannot access them.

Tips (in-app purchases)

If you choose to leave a tip to support development, the purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store. Platal never sees or stores your payment details. Tips are optional and unlock no features — they are simply a way to say thank you.

Children's privacy

Platal does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
