Platal — Support

Platal is a private, on-device personal finance tracker — budgets, expenses, income, recurring payments, and stats, all stored locally on your iPhone.

Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?

Entirely on your device. Platal has no servers and no account — nothing is uploaded anywhere.

How do I back up or move my data?

Go to Settings → Data → Export Backup (you can password-encrypt it). Save the file wherever you like (Files, iCloud Drive, etc.). To restore, use Settings → Data → Import Backup and choose that file.

I changed phones — how do I bring my data over?

Export a backup on the old device, transfer the file (AirDrop or iCloud Drive), then Import it on the new device.

What are the tips?

Optional in-app purchases that support development. They unlock no features — just a thank-you. Payments are handled by Apple; the developer never sees your payment details.

Notifications aren't appearing.

Enable Daily Reminder in Settings, allow notifications when prompted, and make sure notifications for Platal are enabled in iOS Settings → Notifications.
